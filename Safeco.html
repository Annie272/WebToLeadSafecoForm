Policy Coverages

Vehicle with Loss Payee Additional Interest must have Comprehensive Deductible or Collision Deductible Coverage or change the Interest Type.
EAP: includes Emergency expenses, Disabled vehicle- transportation expense, Key lockout-RV, Personal Property Coverage, Roadside assistance.

Roadside Assistance: 1 hour of labor at scene, battery jump start, flat tire change, emergency fuel and fluid delivery (customer pays for cost of fuel/fluid), lockout assistance, towing to nearest repair facility, winching for up to 100 ft from road.

When Roadside Assistance is EAP, Comprehensive Deductible and Collision Deductible can’t be none, and Loss of Use must be 35/per day or above.
